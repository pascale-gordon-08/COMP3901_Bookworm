Why use Bookworm Chat?

Instant Answers

Bookworm Chat provides immediate and accurate answers to queries related to your uploaded textbooks, it will save you valuable time and effort in searching for information.

Relevant and Accurate Information

With Bookworm Chat, you can trust that the information provided is sourced directly from the uploaded textbooks, ensuring accuracy and relevance to your study materials

Reduced Fatigue and Frustration

By eliminating the need to manually search through textbooks, Bookworm Chat reduces fatigue and frustration associated with finding specific information, making your learning process more enjoyable and efficient.


How to use Bookworm Chat

STEP 1: After you sign up/login, select upload textbook in the
navigation bar. Drag or upload your PDF file then press chat.
Alternatively, you may select view library, select a PDF, then press chat.

STEP 2: You can start asking questions related to the textbook's content.
Simply type in your query, or choose from the suggested questions.
Bookworm Chat will process your question to provide you with accurate
and relevant answers.

STEP 3: Bookworm Chat provides a quiz feature to test your knowledge
and assess your understanding of the subject matter. Select 'Take quiz'
in the navigation bar to do so.
